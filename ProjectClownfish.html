Behold! An anemone and its clownfish friends (plus a sphere).
Use the left, right, up, and down arrow keys to move the camera to the left, right, forward, and backward respectively. Use the W, A, S, and D keys to look up, left, down, and right respectively.
Use the M key to change the material of the sphere.
Click the "Show/Hide Grid" button to show or hide the grid. Click the "Gouraud/Phong Shading" button to switch between Gouraud and Phong shading.
Click the "Light Switch" button to switch the light on or off. Click the "Blinn-Phong/Phong Lighting" button to switch between Blinn-Phong and Phong lighting.
Enter x, y, and z coordinates of the desired lamp position, fill in custom RGB values for ambient, diffuse, and specular lighting, and click the respective submit buttons to adjust the lighting in the scene.
